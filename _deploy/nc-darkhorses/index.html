01The honest part

Right now, the organization wears every hat at once.

Nobody sets out to run a 400-kid, 3-time national champion operation off a phone and a personal bank account. It happened because the program got good. Here's the load that has nothing to do with football:

The website

Parents have nowhere official to go for schedules, forms, or answers.

The bank

Fees, gear, and field costs hit your card first and maybe come back.

The group text

One rain delay = 400 messages you send by hand, one thumb at a time.

The paperwork

Rosters, waivers, sizes, and sponsor asks — all living in your head.

None of that wins more games. It just burns out the people who run the program. So let's take it off their plate — and turn most of it into money the program keeps.
