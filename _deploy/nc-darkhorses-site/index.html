The program

More than football. A championship culture.

Since 2020, the Darkhorses have done it the right way — discipline, accountability, and brotherhood first. The wins followed: three AYF Division 2 national titles in four years. We compete at the highest level of youth football while making sure every kid leaves a better student, teammate, and young man.
